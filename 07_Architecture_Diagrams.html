Dual Storage Engine

RDF Triple Store (rdflib)
Namespaces: BFO · IOF · AERO · KB
Nodes: KB:uuid rdf:type AERO:entity_type
Edges: KB:src AERO:relation KB:tgt
Strength: AERO:hasStrength xsd:float

Graph Engine (networkx.DiGraph)
Node attrs: {id, type, label, properties, source_doc}
Edge attrs: {relation, strength, properties}
